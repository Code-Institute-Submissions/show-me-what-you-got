Show Me What You Got

A giant alien head suddenly appears and threatens to eliminate the Earth using giant lasers, from an interglatic talent competition, unless the Earth can show them what we got.
While Rick and Morty are composing a new catchy song to save the world, YOU will have to buy some time for them by playing a matching game.

How to play

You need to find identical cards and match them into pairs.

  • Click on a card to turn it over, then click on another one to see if it matches.
  • If the two cards match, they will stay open. If not, they will both close again.

Be careful! You need to match them all before time's up, or before the Giant Head does!
The Earth's destiny is in your hands!

Game rules

You can choose between solo or combat mode.

Playing solo

You play against a timer. You need to match all the cards before the clock goes to zero.
Any time left at the end of the game will be invaluabe to Rick and Morty in their mission.

Playing combat

You play against the Giant Head. Whoever finds the most matching cards wins.

  • 50:50 chance who goes first
  • If you match a pair, you get to go again
